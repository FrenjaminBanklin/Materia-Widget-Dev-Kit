Materia Widget Developer Kit

Materia widgets are required to have eight icons in sets of two. The largest icon required must be icon-394@2x, and the icon must be 788x788 pixels in size.

This page will display all of the icons your widget requires and allow you to automatically generate icons from the 394x2 size.

Be aware that any icons automatically generated will automatically overwrite any files of that size, if they already exist.
